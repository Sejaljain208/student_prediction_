Academic Performance
  • Assignment Performance: Average scores and completion rates
  • Exam Results: Test scores and grade trends
  • Project Scores: Quality and submission timeliness
  • Result Points: Overall GPA and academic standing
Attendance & Engagement
  • Attendance Rate: Daily presence percentage
  • Days Absent: Total absences and patterns
  • Access Frequency: Platform login and engagement
  • Late Submissions: Assignment punctuality tracking
