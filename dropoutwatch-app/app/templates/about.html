About DropoutWatch

Using Data and AI to Keep Students on Track

Our Mission

DropoutWatch is dedicated to helping educational institutions identify and support at-risk students before they drop out. By combining centralized student data with advanced machine learning, we enable early intervention that saves educational futures.

Every student deserves the opportunity to succeed. Our platform empowers educators with actionable insights, allowing them to provide targeted support where it's needed most.

How It Works

1. Data Collection

Teachers input attendance, grades, activities, fees, and counseling notes

2. Feature Extraction

System calculates 12 key features per student automatically

3. Risk Prediction

Random Forest model classifies students into Low/Medium/High risk

4. Alert & Intervene

Automated alerts trigger intervention by counselors and teachers
