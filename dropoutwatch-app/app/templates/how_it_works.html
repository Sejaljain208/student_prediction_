How DropoutWatch Works

A comprehensive 6-step process combining data science and AI to predict and prevent student dropouts

1

Data Collection

Comprehensive student data is collected from multiple sources:

  • Attendance Records: Daily presence tracking
  • Academic Performance: Grades, assignments, exams
  • Assignment Completion: Submission rates and scores
  • Fee Status: Payment history and outstanding amounts
  • Extracurricular Activities: Participation levels
  • Counseling Notes: Behavioral and emotional observations
2

Data Preprocessing

Raw data is cleaned, validated, and transformed into meaningful features:

Data Cleaning

Remove duplicates, handle missing values, validate formats

Feature Engineering

Calculate percentages, trends, and derived metrics

Normalization

Scale features for optimal model performance

12 Key Features Extracted: Attendance % Avg Assignment Score Avg Exam Score Assignment Completion Rate Extracurricular Participation Outstanding Fees Ratio Counseling Concern Level Days Absent Grade Trend Late Submissions Behavioral Incidents Project Scores
3

Machine Learning Model

We evaluated multiple ML algorithms and selected the best performer:

Model Accuracy Precision Recall Status
Random Forest 90% 88% 92% ✓ Selected
Support Vector Machine (SVM) 85% 83% 87% Tested
Logistic Regression 82% 80% 84% Tested
Why Random Forest?
  • Handles non-linear relationships effectively
  • Provides feature importance rankings
  • Robust against overfitting with ensemble approach
  • Works well with imbalanced datasets
4

Risk Score Generation

The model generates a comprehensive risk assessment:

Low Risk

0-33%

Student is on track, minimal intervention needed

Medium Risk

34-66%

Requires monitoring and support

High Risk

67-100%

Immediate intervention required

Contributing Factors Identified:

The system identifies specific reasons for the risk level, such as:

Low attendance (<75%) Poor grades (<40%) High fees overdue Low assignment completion Counseling concerns No extracurricular activities
5

Dashboard Visualization

Results are displayed in intuitive, interactive dashboards:

Risk Distribution Charts

Visual breakdown of student risk levels across classes

Student Lists

Sortable, filterable tables with risk indicators

Trend Analysis

Attendance and grade trends over time

Individual Profiles

Detailed student views with all contributing factors

6

Automated Alerts & Intervention

Stakeholders are automatically notified when students need help:

Email Alerts

Sent to parents and counselors with detailed risk information and action steps

SMS Notifications

Quick text alerts via Twilio for urgent high-risk cases

Counselor Queue

High-risk students automatically added to intervention queue

Early Intervention Impact:

Studies show early detection and intervention can improve student retention rates by 15-25%
