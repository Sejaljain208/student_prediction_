Core Capabilities

Comprehensive Early Warning System

Our platform integrates multiple data sources and applies advanced machine learning to provide actionable insights for educators, counselors, and administrators.

📊

Centralized Student Profiles

Aggregate all student data in one unified profile: attendance records (flagging <75%), grades, assignment completions, extracurricular participation, financial dues, and counseling notes. No more scattered information.

🤖

ML-Based Risk Scoring

Our Random Forest ensemble model analyzes complex, high-dimensional student data to compute accurate risk scores. Each student is classified as Low Risk, Medium Risk, or High Risk.

📈

Real-Time Educator Dashboard

Interactive, responsive web dashboard provides teachers and mentors with instant visibility into each student's risk category and contributing factors. Monitor trends, track interventions, and make data-driven decisions.

🔔

Automated Alert System

When a student reaches Medium or High risk, automatic SMS and email notifications are sent to teachers, parents, and counselors. Early alerts enable timely interventions before it's too late.

🔍

Multi-Factor Analysis

Analyze academic performance, attendance patterns, behavioral indicators, socioeconomic background, and qualitative teacher feedback. Our system considers all known dropout predictors for comprehensive assessment.

📉

Interactive Visualizations

Charts, progress bars, and trend graphs help educators quickly grasp each student's status. Visualize attendance over time, grade distributions, risk breakdowns, and intervention effectiveness.

Our Process

How DropoutWatch Works

A simple, four-step process that transforms raw student data into actionable insights.

1

Data Integration

Collect and centralize student data from multiple sources: attendance systems, grade books, LMS platforms, counseling records, and financial systems.

2

ML Analysis

Our Random Forest model processes the data, identifying patterns and correlations across academic, behavioral, and socioeconomic factors.

3

Risk Classification

Each student receives a risk score and classification (Low/Medium/High) with detailed explanations of contributing factors.

4

Intervention & Monitoring

Stakeholders receive alerts, access dashboards, and implement targeted interventions. Continuous monitoring tracks progress and adjusts risk levels.
